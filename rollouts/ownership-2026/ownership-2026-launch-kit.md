# Ownership 2026 Launch Kit

## Campaign

**Name:** Become An Owner In 2026  
**Theme:** The Ownership Freeze  
**Core line:** Break out of rented platforms. Own your web.

## Core Message

Most people are moving fast with AI, social media, and quick design tools, but they still do not own the foundation of what they are building.

Ownership means:

- your domain
- your website
- your brand files
- your offer page
- your contact flow
- your email list
- your payment path
- your follow-up system

## Launch Objective

Turn the YouTube video into Episode 01 of a larger ownership campaign, then use real client proof and sold demo sites to push people toward OWNYOURWEB and ShopNasGFX.
